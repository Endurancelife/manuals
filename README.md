# Endurancelife Participant Manuals

Welcome to the official digital hub for Endurancelife event rules, safety protocols, and course matrices. To ensure you have a safe and successful race weekend, please review your event manual carefully.

### 🗺️ Select Your Event Manual
Click on the **"Select Event"** dropdown menu in the top-right corner of your screen to choose your specific race directory.

Once selected:
* Your sidebar will instantly populate with your event's specific timeline.
* The search index bar will automatically filter to scan only your chosen event's layout rules.

---

### 🛑 Core Race Rules (Applies to All Events)

Our trail events are staged across highly protected, environmentally sensitive national landscapes. The following core mandates apply across every single Endurancelife course line:

* **Zero Litter Tolerance:** Dropping trash anywhere along the trail results in an immediate disqualification. 
* **Strict Checkpoint Cut-offs:** Medical crews and marshals enforce absolute cut-off metrics at designated aid stations for athlete safety. 
* **Mandatory Kit Compliance:** Kit checks are randomly performed at registration and out on the trail layout. Failure to carry your required safety gear will freeze your timing chip.
