# Exmoor Ultra Participant Manual

Welcome to the official participant manual for the Exmoor Ultra. Staged along the dramatic, brutal, and breathtaking coastal paths of North Devon, this course features steep climbs, rugged single-track trails, and stunning coastal vistas. 

Please review the event timelines, safety requirements, and course metrics below to ensure a successful race weekend.

---

## 📅 Event Schedule & Registration

### Friday Evening
* **17:00 - 19:00:** Early Registration & Mandatory Kit Checks at the Event Village.

### Saturday (Race Day)
* **06:30 - 07:45:** Race Day Registration Open.
* **07:45:** Mandatory Pre-Race Safety Briefing (All participants must attend).
* **08:00:** **Exmoor Ultra Race Start.**
* **08:30:** Exmoor Marathon & Half Marathon Starts.

---

## 🏃 Course Details & Cut-offs

The Exmoor Ultra is a demanding trail environment. Checkpoints are spaced strategically to support you, but you must maintain a steady pace to clear checkpoints before your designated cut-off markers.

[Cut-off Regulations](/_shared/global-copy.md ':include :fragment=cutoff-boilerplate')

### Checkpoint Matrix

Below are the exact distances, safety facilities, and closing times for the Exmoor layout:

| Checkpoint | Distance Covered | Safety Facilities Available | Cut-off Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Start Line** | 0.0 km | Toilets, Water, Medical | 08:00 AM |
| **Checkpoint 1** | 14.2 km | Water, Energy Drinks, Snacks | 11:15 AM |
| **Checkpoint 2** | 29.5 km | Hot Drinks, Food, Medical, Toilets | 02:30 PM |
