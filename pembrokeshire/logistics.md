# Travel, Parking & Arrival Logistics

## 3.1 Event Parking
Please drive carefully when travelling to and from the venue. 

* **Location & Navigation:** Follow event signs close to **SA62 3JY**.
* **Permit Requirements:** Parking permits must be bought online before race day. Upon purchasing, you will receive an email confirmation. Please have this confirmation ready on your phone to show the car parking team as you approach the gate to prevent queues. There is no need to print anything or display a permit on your windscreen.
* **Payment:** We cannot accept cash on-the-day. Parking costs **£3** for the full day.
* **Suitability:** This is field parking suitable for standard cars and high vehicles (such as camper vans). 
* **Low Undercarriage Vehicles:** If your vehicle has particularly low ground clearance, we recommend parking directly at the nearby **Broad Haven (Marine Rd) car park** (separate local charges apply).
* **Drop-offs & Pickups:** Drop-offs and collections under 1 hour are free.

## 3.2 Coach Transfers & Shuttles
Optional morning coaches are available to transport runners from the finish area parking outside Little Haven village to the remote start lines for the 10K and Half Marathon classes.

* **Cost:** **[TBC: Coach Price]** (Please book online in advance to reserve your seat).
* **Departure Times:**
  * **10K Coach Departure:** 08:40
  * **Half Marathon Coach Departure:** 09:55
* **Preparation:** Fill your water bottles and use the toilets before boarding.
* **Baggage Note:** You can leave one item of warm clothing or waterproofs with the team at the start line (not on the coach). This will be transported back to the finish line and be ready for you after you cross it.

---

## 3.3 Start & Finish Layouts
* **Ultra Marathon & Marathon:** These are looped events. Both categories start and finish in the central event village at **Little Haven village, SA62 3JX**.
* **10K & Half Marathon:** These follow linear paths. 
  * **10K Start Line:** Located in a remote spot on the coastline outside the village of Marloes. If dropped off by family or friends, arrive by 09:00 latest (or park in Marloes village and walk). Pull-in area is highly limited and reserved primarily for coaches; drop-off vehicles must exit immediately. No toilets are available at this start line.
  * **Half Marathon Start Line:** Positioned at the edge of Dale airfield. If dropped off, head to Dale Beach Car Park where crew will direct you along a 15-minute walk to the start line.

## 3.5 Travel & Public Transport
The venue is easiest to reach by car via the road network, but public transport can be combined with a local taxi.

* **International Airport:** Cardiff Airport
* **Regional Airport:** Cardiff Airport
* **Mainline Railway Station:** Swansea
* **Mainline Coach Terminal:** Swansea
