You must carry the required safety equipment at all times from start to finish. Sharing kit between runners is not permitted.

## 6.2 Recommended Clothing & Footwear
Our events take place in remote, exposed coastal areas with little natural shelter. Weather conditions can change rapidly. Do not underestimate how cold and wet it could be.

We strongly recommend carrying or wearing:
* **Trail Running Shoes:** Highly recommended for safety and grip on technical trails. Standard road running shoes lack the necessary tread.
* **Apparel:** Waterproof jacket and trousers, warm layers, sunglasses, and sun cream. Paths may be thick with seasonal foliage or nettles, so consider your clothing choices carefully.
* **Accessories:** Anti-chafing lubricant, your reusable drinking cup, and a small, sealable plastic bag to protect your phone and valuables from moisture.

---

## 6.3 Kit Inspections & Penalties
Our team may inspect your mandatory safety equipment at any time. These checks typically happen at an undisclosed location on the trail or immediately as you cross the finish line.

Carrying the correct kit is essential for your safety. **Failing to carry a water vessel, a windproof jacket, or your mobile phone when mandated will result in immediate disqualification.** For other missing items, a 10-minute time penalty or disqualification will be enforced depending on the item missing.
