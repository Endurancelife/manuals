# Age Limits, Classes & Awards

## 9.1 Minimum Entry Ages
Participant age thresholds are calculated based on your exact age on race day:
* **10K Category:** Minimum age is 16, or ages 14 and 15 if accompanied by a registered adult (18+) for the entire course.
* **Half Marathon Category:** Minimum age is 16.
* **Marathon or Ultra Marathon Categories:** Minimum age is 18.

---

## 9.2 Race Timing & Results
Event timing is done by digitally scanning your unique race number. Live finish-line results will be broadcast in real-time onto our online results portal. Course checkpoint split times will be verified and uploaded to our website results database within 12 hours of the event's conclusion.

---

## 9.3 Changing Distance Mid-Race
You will only be included in the results of the specific distance category you start in on race morning. For example, if you register and start within the Ultra category but choose to stop early after the Marathon loop, you will be classified as a DNF on the leaderboards; you cannot claim a Marathon finish.

If you miss your start wave, you may be permitted a later start at the discretion of race control, but you will forfeit prize eligibility and standard cut-off times will still apply strictly to your progress.

---

## 9.4 Prizes & Podiums
* **Open Divisions:** Trophies and prizes from Inov-8, Luchos Dillitos, UltraRunner Magazine, and GU Energy are presented on-site to the top 3 overall Male and Female finishers in each distance class.
* **Age Categories:** Rankings are tracked across these brackets: Under 20, Open, V45, V50, V55, V60, V65, and V70.
* **Dog Participants:** Running with a dog automatically excludes participants from overall podium positions or age-group team awards.

*The Competition Rule:* To preserve competitive integrity, age-category prizes are only issued if active competition is verified. **A minimum of two participants within a specific age category must start the race for an award to be active.** If exactly three runners start within a bracket, only the top two finishers receive prizes. If exactly two runners start, only the outright winner receives an award.

Podium ceremonies for overall top finishers will be held at the main marquee throughout the afternoon. We cannot present age-category awards on-the-day, as deep-field results verification cannot be completed thoroughly within the race-day timeframe. All verified age-category winners will be emailed the week following the event to arrange distribution.
