# Pembrokeshire 2026 Participant Manual

## 1. Welcome & Initial Preparation

### 1.1 Welcome & Series Ethos
The UK coastline is spectacular, and we are proud to showcase it. Each event in our national series has its own character and unique challenges, but they all share incredible natural beauty and breathtaking scenery.

Our philosophy is simple: *It is all about the journey. Never give up.*

---

### 1.2 What this Guide Covers
This guide tells you what to expect, details your safety responsibilities, and provides a preparation checklist.

It does not cover booking account changes or entry amendments. For transfers, deferrals, or account updates, please visit the event webpage.

---

### 1.3 Pre-Event Checklist
Please complete these actions before you arrive at the venue:
* **Read this manual** carefully to understand the event rules.
* **Save the Event Safety Number** into your mobile phone contacts (**07852 977461**).
* **Sort your gear and nutrition**, and fill your water bottles.
* **Check the Updates tab** on the event webpage for real-time weather and race updates.
* **Fully charge your phone.** It is a mandatory safety item and you will need it to retrieve your race number via SMS verification.
