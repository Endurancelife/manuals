# Pre-Race Administration & Base Operations

## 2.1 Entries & Changes
All entries must be made in advance online. We do not accept on-the-day registrations, distance category changes, or runner substitutions at the venue on race day. For urgent enquiries before event week, email us at `support@endurancelife.com`.

---

## 2.2 Online Check-In
You must check in online approximately two weeks before the event. This system removes on-the-day queues so you can focus on your race preparation. During online check-in, you will verify your emergency contact details (ICE) and confirm you have watched the mandatory safety video.

---

## 2.3 Safety Video & Briefing
Watching the online safety video is mandatory and forms part of your check-in. There is no physical safety briefing on race day. However, we will broadcast key safety reminders over the PA system, and our crew will be on hand to answer questions.

---

## 2.4 Medical Details
You must disclose any pre-existing medical conditions and prescriptions in your online account profile prior to event week. If you have any last-minute amendments, please provide these in writing to the medical team at race control on race morning.

---

## 4.1 Collecting your Race Number
Race numbers are collected on-site on event day (they are not posted out ahead of time). 

* **Ultra and Marathon Runners:** Collect your bib at the main marquee by the start line.
* **10K and Half Marathon Runners:** Collect your bib as you board the coach (if taking the optional coach transport) or directly at the start line (if making your own way there).

* **Verification:** Bring your fully charged mobile phone to show the SMS text confirming your race number.
* **Visibility Rule:** Fix your paper race number securely to your front (stomach or right leg) at all times. Do not fold or cut it. It must remain clearly visible at checkpoints for scanning; please unzip or temporarily adjust any jackets or layers as you approach to ensure a quick transition.

---

## 4.2 Event Village Amenities (Little Haven)
* **Endurancelife Café:** Operating throughout the day serving hot and cold drinks, porridge, pasta pots, bananas, and a selection of snacks.
* **Post-Race Massage:** Available all afternoon at £12 for a 10-minute session. Book online in advance to guarantee a slot and save 10%.
* **Toilets:** Full facilities are provided at the main event village base.
* **Changing Tents:** There are no changing tents or shower facilities on-site.

> **Logistical Tip:** If travelling with other participants, it is prudent to give the car keys to whoever is likely to finish first so they can access warm clothes immediately.

---

## 4.3 Baggage Storage
* A secure bag drop area is provided at the main event village base for your personal kit.
* **Requirements:** Bags must be waterproof, securely zipped, under **30 litres** in size, and clearly labelled with your name and race number.
