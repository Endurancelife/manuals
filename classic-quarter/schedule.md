# Event Schedule

Please note that all times are strictly enforced. Ensure you arrive at registration with ample time to complete the mandatory kit check.

## Friday (Pre-Race)

* **16:00** - Basecamp Opens (Camping available)
* **18:00 to 21:00** - Early Registration & Kit Check

## Saturday (Race Day)

* **05:00** - Late Registration Opens
* **05:45** - Race Briefing (Mandatory for all runners)
* **06:00** - Race Start
* **20:00** - Course Officially Closes
