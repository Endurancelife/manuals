# Rules & Regulations

All participants must adhere to the following rules to ensure a safe, fair, and environmentally responsible race.

## 1. Route Navigation
You must follow the South West Coast Path at all times. Cutting switchbacks or diverting through private property will result in immediate disqualification.

## 2. Checkpoints & Cut-offs
You must check in with the marshals at every designated checkpoint. If you miss a cut-off time, you will be withdrawn from the race. Marshals' decisions are final.

## 3. Environmental Policy
Endurancelife operates a strict leave-no-trace policy. Any participant found littering on the course will be disqualified and banned from future events. Please retain all gel wrappers.
