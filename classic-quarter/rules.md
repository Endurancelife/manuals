# Rules & Regulations

Please read the following rules carefully. The safety of our runners and the preservation of the trail are our highest priorities. Failure to comply may result in time penalties or immediate disqualification.

<details>
<summary><b>1. Race Numbers & Tracking</b></summary>

* Your race number must be **visible at all times** on your front. Do not attach it to your backpack.
* Do not fold, cut, or alter the race number in any way.
* If you are issued a GPS tracker, it must be attached to the top of your pack with a clear view of the sky.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>2. Mandatory Kit Compliance</b></summary>

* You must carry the entire mandatory kit for your specific distance from the start line to the very finish line.
* **Spot checks** will be carried out on the course and at the finish line. 
* Missing items will result in an immediate time penalty or disqualification, depending on the severity and weather conditions.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>3. Checkpoint Cut-Off Times</b></summary>

* Cut-off times are strictly enforced. You must *leave* the checkpoint before the cut-off time, not just arrive at it.
* Marshals do not have the authority to extend cut-off times.
* If you miss a cut-off, you will be withdrawn from the race and transported to the finish line.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>4. Environmental Respect & Littering</b></summary>

* We operate a strict **leave-no-trace** policy.
* Dropping litter of any kind (including torn gel wrapper tabs) on the trail will result in **instant disqualification** and a potential ban from future Endurancelife events.
* Please hold all trash and use the bins provided at the checkpoints.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>5. Medical & Dropping Out</b></summary>

* If you need to retire from the race, you **must** inform the nearest checkpoint marshal or call Race HQ immediately.
* Never leave the course and go home without informing the event team, or we will have to initiate emergency search and rescue protocols.
* The medical team's decision to remove a runner from the course for health reasons is final.

</details>
