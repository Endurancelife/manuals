# Classic Quarter: Race Overview

The definitive guide to the Classic Quarter. Prepare yourself for relentless, unforgiving terrain across the stark beauty of the Cornish coast.

## The Challenge

The Classic Quarter is notorious for its rapidly changing weather systems and deep coastal undulations. You are expected to be self-sufficient and capable of navigating using map and compass or GPS under extreme fatigue.

## Distance & Elevation

* **Distance:** 44 Miles
* **Total Ascent:** +6,500ft
* **Cut-Off Time:** 14 Hours
