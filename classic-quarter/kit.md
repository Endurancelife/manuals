# Mandatory Kit

The weather on the coast can change rapidly. The mandatory kit is for your absolute safety. Kit checks will take place at registration. Missing items will result in a DNS (Did Not Start).

#### **Quarter Marathon**

* 500ml Water capacity
* Fully charged mobile phone (with race HQ number saved)
* Whistle
* Foil survival blanket

#### **Ultra Marathon**

* 1000ml Water capacity
* Fully charged mobile phone (with race HQ number saved)
* Whistle
* Foil survival blanket
* Waterproof jacket with taped seams
* Headtorch with spare batteries
* Base layer top (long sleeves)

## Footwear Recommendations

We highly recommend aggressive trail shoes. Road shoes are strictly prohibited due to the mud and technical descents on the coastal path.
